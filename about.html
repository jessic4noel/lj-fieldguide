A Field Guide to ЖЖ

curated by jess_ru · POLISCI 227R

Summary All entries Sources About

Project

A Field Guide to ЖЖ is an interactive web exhibition styled as a reflection of LiveJournal (LJ, ЖЖ), Russia's accidental public sphere from 1999 to 2024. The landing page is laid out as a LiveJournal "friends feed" with four entries that open into four "rooms" Adoption, Art and Culture, Public Sphere, and Capture. Each maps an era of LJ's rise and capture in Russia.

Curator

Jessica Badiny is an undergraduate studying International Relations at Stanford University. This project was developed for POLISCI 227R, Russian Politics since 1991, Spring 2026.

Website Details

Each room presents a historically significant LiveJournal post as its central artifact, with brief curatorial commentary appearing as threaded "comments" beneath. The rooms mirror the form of the platform itself, and all original Russian-language posts are accompanied by English translations. A full bibliography of sources is available on the Sources page.
