The Public Square

posted by: jess_ru

mood: charged  ·  music: Bolotnaya Square, snow on a microphone

tags: navalny, protest, bots, journalism

Artist statement. The room where the literary public sphere learns to act politically — and where its adversaries learn its terrain. Anton Nossik's daily column showed LJ as professional infrastructure; Alexei Navalny's 2011 election-fraud posts showed it as mobilization infrastructure. Sanovich, Stukal, & Tucker (2018), Appendix B, is the empirical anchor for the moment the regime moved from ignoring to contesting the platform.
